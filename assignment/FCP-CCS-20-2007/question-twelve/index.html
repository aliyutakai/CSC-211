About the A.Y.M Shafa International hotel

A.Y.M Shafa International hotel is an exclusive hotel comprising of just 90 guest rooms and suites plus four oplent beachfront villas. it is incredibly private with a feeling of expansive space and acts as a private escape for guests to enjoy spectacular views of the hotel. Unwind in your luxury room, suite or private beachfront villa beneath swaying palms by our mesmerising infinity pool, framed by the romance of Andalusian architecture. Wander through the immaculate gardens of our elegant estate, towards our private beach and marina, gently lapped by the deep blue waters of the Arabian Gulf. Guests are invited to stroll along the private beach, bask in the sun on oversized daybeds by the swimming pool, relax in one of the six air contioned poolside Cabana Suites with indoor and outdoor lounge areas LCD screens wet bar, en-suite bathroom and shower or unwind in the adjacent all suite A.Y.M Shafa International hotel.
