Welcome to FALALI Memorial College

FALALI Memorial College is an academic institution like no other.

FALALI Memorial College is a Private higher institution located in Kano, the capital city of Kano State in northeast Nigeria. The institution was established in the year 2016, with the intention of it's becoming one of the country's principal higher-education institutions. It enrolls about 2000 students in it's combined programs, which include Social Science, Criminology, English, Mathematics, Computer Science, Cyber Security, Economics, Accouting, Business Management and Administration. The FALALI Memorial College exits to cultivate learning, develop knowledge, and foster free exchange of ideas. The institution will focus on a strong tradition of distinguished scholarship, research, teaching, and service that is grounded in a commitment to increasing and disseminating scientific, humanistic, and social knowledge for the benefit of larger society. In this, FMC will strive to be a major research university with extensive graduate programs that are also dedicated to outstanding undergraduate and professional education. The institution faculties and departments will be committed to the intellectual, cultural, and ethical development of students as citizens, scholars, and professionals. FMC graduates will be prepared to contribute a global society that requires leaders with creativity, integrity, and a dedication to service. Finally, FMC is committed to ideas that will make it to be recognized around the world as one of the best institutions of higher education.
